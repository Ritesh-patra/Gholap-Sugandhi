About Us

Fragrance of Faith
Since 1952

Since 1952, Gholap Sugandhi has been a trusted name in fragrance and devotion, rooted in the heart of Mandai, Pune. Our journey began with a simple vision — to create authentic aromatic experiences that connect tradition with spirituality.

We are proud manufacturers of Keshar Ashtagandh and offer 30+ incense stick brands loved across India. From rich Masala Agarbatti to pure perfume-based fragrances, every product is crafted to uplift your senses and bring peace.

We also specialize in Dhoop cups and sticks and are trusted suppliers to renowned temples like Shirdi Sansthan, Udipi Krishna Mandir, and Datta Devasthan Ahmednagar. For us, fragrance is not just a product — it is a tradition, a prayer, and a bond with generations.
