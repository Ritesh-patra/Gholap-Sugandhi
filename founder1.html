Mr. Shashikant Gholap

Honouring a Visionary & His Legacy

The Scent of Devotion and Discovery

Nestled in the heart of Mandai, Pune, Gholap Sugandhi has carried the fragrance of faith through generations. Founded in 1952, the brand began with a simple yet profound vision — to create pure, soulful scents that become part of every prayer and every home.

Driven by a spirit of customer obsession and a passion for fragrances, Shashikant dedicated his life to perfecting blends that speak to the heart. With a research‑driven mindset and strong business acumen, he travelled across India and the Middle East, gathering rare essences such as **Oud, Sandalwood, and Saffron** to craft original formulations that became Gholap Sugandhi’s unique intellectual property.

"Innovation is respect for tradition, delivered with modern excellence."

For over seven decades, we have devoted ourselves to crafting authentic aromatic experiences that bridge tradition and spirituality. His creations — from the rich **Masala Agarbattis** to the divine **Kesar Ashtagandha** — reflect both artistry and discipline.

Our fragrances are trusted by devotees and temples alike, including Shirdi Sansthan, Udipi Krishna Mandir, and Datta Devasthan Ahmednagar. Beyond incense, our dhoop cups and sticks continue the same commitment to authenticity and spiritual connection.

Looking Ahead — Continuing the Legacy

As we look to the future, Gholap Sugandhi remains guided by the same spirit that began in 1952. Today, the next generation carries forward Shashikant Gholap’s vision with renewed creativity, ensuring that the fragrance of faith continues to inspire hearts for generations to come.

The Visionary Founder

Mr. Dhananjay Gholap

Every great business has a story, and at the heart of ours is Mr. Dhananjay Gholap. More than 50 years ago, he began a journey that was less about building a company and more about nurturing relationships, trust, and values. With quiet determination and unwavering commitment, he has guided the business through decades of change, ensuring it grows while staying true to its roots.

Mr. Gholap has always believed that success lies in the details—whether it’s managing supplier relations single-handedly, overseeing day-to-day execution, or listening closely to customers long after a sale is made. His ability to connect with people, understand their needs, and ensure their satisfaction has created a bond that goes beyond transactions.

"For generations, customers have returned not just for the products, but for the warmth and reliability that he embodies."

His story is one of resilience, care, and dedication—a legacy that continues to inspire the business today. Under his guidance, efficiency and customer satisfaction are not just goals, but promises kept, making him the true cornerstone of our journey.
